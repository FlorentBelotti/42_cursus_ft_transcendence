Site Rules

Last updated: April 2, 2025

Welcome to pong.ovh! This site is a project created by Théo Gérardin, Romain Lambert, and Florent Belotti, three French students. To ensure a pleasant experience for everyone, we have established the following rules. By using this site, you agree to abide by them.

1. Purpose of the site

pong.ovh is a non-commercial website intended to showcase a student project: an online pong game with a ranking system. Any use outside this purpose is prohibited.

2. User behavior

You agree to:

  • Not use offensive, discriminatory, vulgar, or illegal usernames.
  • Not post illegal, violent, pornographic, or otherwise inappropriate content.

3. Anti-cheating rules

To ensure fair play, it is strictly forbidden to:

  • Use software, scripts, or tools to cheat or alter the site's functionality.
  • Exploit bugs or vulnerabilities to gain an unfair advantage.
  • Create multiple accounts to bypass rules or manipulate scores (e.g., Elo).

4. Consequences of violations

If you violate these rules, we reserve the right to:

  • Edit or remove your username if deemed inappropriate.
  • Suspend or delete your account without prior notice.
  • Block your access to the site in the case of serious or repeated offenses.

No compensation will be provided for deleted accounts.

5. Liability

We do our best to keep the site running smoothly, but we do not guarantee it will be free of bugs or interruptions. You use the site at your own risk.

6. Contact

If you want to report an issue or ask a question about these rules, contact us at contact@pong.ovh.
