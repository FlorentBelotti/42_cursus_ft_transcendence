Data Protection Policy (GDPR)

Last updated: April 2, 2025

We are Théo Gérardin, Romain Lambert, and Florent Belotti, three French students responsible for the pong.ovh website. This site is non-commercial and not affiliated with any company. We are committed to protecting your personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) of the European Union.

1. Data collected

We may collect certain personal data when you use our site, such as:

  • Your email address (if you create an account or contact us).
  • A username or identifier (if applicable).
  • Technical data automatically collected during your browsing.

If you do not create an account or interact with the site, no personally identifiable data is intentionally collected.

2. Purpose of processing

The collected data is used solely to:

  • Ensure the site functions correctly.
  • Allow access to your account, if applicable.
  • Respond to any inquiries or messages.

We do not sell your data or use it for commercial purposes.

3. Legal basis

Data processing is based on:

  • Your consent (e.g., when creating an account).
  • Our legitimate interest in operating the site and providing a smooth user experience.

4. Data retention

Your data is retained as long as necessary for the stated purposes, or until you request its deletion. If you delete your account, your data will be erased within a reasonable time.

5. Your rights

In accordance with the GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: View the data we hold about you.
  • Right to rectification: Correct your data if it is inaccurate.
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data.
  • Right to restriction: Limit the use of your data in certain cases.
  • Right to object: Object to the processing of your data.
  • Right to data portability: Obtain your data in a readable format.

To exercise these rights, go to the "Account" page and use the "Delete account" option. For any other request, contact us at contact@pong.ovh.

6. Data security

We implement technical measures to protect your data from unauthorized access or accidental loss. However, no system is foolproof, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Data transfer

Your data is not transferred outside the European Union.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about your data or this policy, you can contact us at contact@pong.ovh.

9. Supervisory authority

If you believe your rights are not being respected, you can contact the French Data Protection Authority (CNIL): www.cnil.fr.
